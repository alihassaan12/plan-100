Our Vision

Plan100 is an initiative of Hello World Technologies, aimed at creating 100 IT companies in Rahim Yar Khan, a rural area in Pakistan, by 2023.

“From Startup to Ecosystem.”

Our Vision is to create a Thriving and Diverse IT Startup Ecosystem that drives Innovation and creates long-term Value for society. Through Plan 100, IT startups will have access to the resources they need to Scale & Succeed, and they will be able to Collaborate and Learn from each other, further Strengthening the Community.

An Initiative by Hello World Technologies.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to Empower and Support IT Startups by providing them with the necessary resources, including Funding, Mentorship, Networking Opportunities, & Access to a community of Like-Minded Entrepreneurs. The goal of 100 Plan is to create an environment that fosters innovation and growth in the IT industry, Ultimately Leading to the creation of Successful and Sustainable Businesses.
